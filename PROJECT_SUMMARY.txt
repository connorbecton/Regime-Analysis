📈 MODEL METHODOLOGY
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

1. Calculate 13-week momentum for all ETFs
2. Compute spreads (Long basket - Short basket)
3. Normalize to z-scores (rolling 252-week window)
4. Discretize z-scores (-2, -1, 0, +1, +2)
5. Weight discrete scores by signal weights
6. Sum all weighted scores = Composite Score
7. Smooth composite with EWMA (6-week span)
8. Classify: EWMA ≥ +7 → Defensive, ≤ -7 → Risk-On, else Neutral
